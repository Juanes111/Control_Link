About Us Who are we

Control Link was established in 2007 as an Engineering Service provider specializing in system Maintenance of ABB control systems such as Advant Master, ABB Bailey and ABB MOD300.

Additionally we provide consulting on projects, Contract Audit and Dimensioning of Equipment for implementation of Control Strategies in Oil & Gas Industry.

Who are we

We currently provide Controls Engineering Services to the Energy and Pulp and Paper industries thus expanding our knowledge and experience in these fields. Our goal is to be present in DCS Migration projects to new technologies and continue our Services and Maintenance in the field of Automation and Process Control. Additionally, our staff is highly experienced in developing software and hardware solutions for the integration of information from the field to Administrative Tools that facilitate the analysis and subsequent decision-making at the Management level.

Mission

We are a worldwide company that provides customized Software development for Automation and Control Systems covering Oil & Gas, Energy and general Industry.

Vision

Be recognized in the industry as the leaders in software and control automation development, with our commitment in supporting the needs and continuous process improvement of our customers.

Value Promise

Ensuring Quality of Service we give to our customers the longest lasting and most cost effective solutions in the field of Automation and Control.

Health and Safety at Work and Environment Policy

CONTROL LINK a professional organization providing Engineering Services in Automation and Control, is committed to compliance with all Workplace Health, Safety and Environmental laws and regulations. Through the management of financial resources and human talent we will develop activities that will identify and eliminate occupational hazards that are present in the workplace, with the goal of improving the quality of work of our employees , suppliers, subcontractors, and persons of interest involved in our automation services. We will generate and maintain a culture of preventing accidents, personal injuries and illnesses; and preventing property and environmental damage.
